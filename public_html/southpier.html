One of the most popular things to do in Capital City is walking around the Southpier! With tons of things to do for people of all ages, this attraction is a must visit for every tourist.

What is the Southpier?

Southpier is located on the Southern beach of Capital City. Southpier is a recent addition to the lovely city and attracts hundreds of tourists each year. There are tons of fun things to do on Southpier after a day at the beach. Ever get tired of being in a hot sun and need a break? The Southpier has comfy hammocks under the shade of palm trees!

Food, Fruit, and Fun

Southpier offers a lovely view of the ocean while providing an abundance of delicious food options which you can find on our Foods page. Find seafood, fruit stands, amazing desserts and much more! Enjoy cute little food stands or fancy sit down restaurants with an outlook of the beach! Ever had a fruit kebab? Make your own at Kebab It, name a fruit and you will have it!

All the Fun!

If you ever need a relaxing walk, think about how amazing a long walk across a beauiful prier would be! While walking, check out the many attractions! There is a huge arcade offering many different games for young kids. Next to the Pier is the ski shop! Offering several different fun activites such as jet skiing, paddle boarding, and floaties for the kids.
